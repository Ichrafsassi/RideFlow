Q: Why should I use this app?
A: RideFlow is fast, safe, and reliable. Our platform combines smart dispatching, live traffic data, and professional drivers to deliver efficient trips at transparent prices.
Q: How do you recruit drivers? How can I know that my driver is trustworthy?
A: Every RideFlow driver completes identity verification, background checks, vehicle validation, and quality onboarding before they can accept rides.
Q: How do I request a ride?
A: Open the RideFlow app, set your pickup and destination, choose your ride tier, and confirm. Your driver will arrive shortly.
Q: How much does it cost?
A: RideFlow fares are based on time, distance, and real-time demand. You always see an upfront estimate before confirming your trip.
Q: How does it work?
A: Our dispatch engine matches nearby available drivers with riders using proximity, ETA, and route efficiency. The platform runs on serverless AWS services so it can scale quickly while staying reliable.
Q: What if I have a complaint about my driver?
A: RideFlow is a customer obsessed company. We value each customer and want to ensure a positive experience. Therefore, we’ve staffed our customer service team with serverless chatbots that are available 24/7 to assist you.
Q: How do I cancel my ride?
A: Tap the "Cancel Ride" button in the RideFlow app.
Q: Can I use RideFlow internationally?
A: Yes, you can use RideFlow in most countries except for Antarctica, Cuba, Sudan, Iran, North Korea, Syria and any other country designated by the United States Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control.
Q: How do I pay for my ride?
A: After creating a RideFlow account, add your preferred payment method such as credit card, debit card, or digital wallet. After your trip completes, the fare is charged automatically.
Q: How many passengers can one ride take?
A: Capacity depends on the ride option you choose. Standard rides support up to 4 passengers, and larger options support more where available.
Q: What if I lose an item during my ride?
A: Use the in-app help flow to report a lost item. We notify your driver and help coordinate recovery where possible.
Q: How do I share my route information with someone else?
A: During your ride, you can share your route and ETA with someone else using the RideFlow app. Tap the "Share Route" button and select a contact.
Q: How do I rate my driver?
A: After your ride completes, rate your driver directly in the app. Your feedback improves quality and helps reward top-performing drivers.
Q: What if my driver does not match the app details?
A: The driver and vehicle information in your app should match what arrives. If not, do not enter the vehicle and report the issue immediately.
Q: Can I use Concur with RideFlow?
A: Yes, you can connect your Concur profile to the RideFlow app so you can track business trips made on RideFlow.
Q: Can I request a specific driver?
A: You cannot request a specific driver, but you can choose the ride category that best matches your needs.
Q: Why do you charge a service fee?
A: The service fee helps cover platform operations, safety features, and continuous improvements to RideFlow.

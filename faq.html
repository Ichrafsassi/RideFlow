Frequently Asked Questions

Everything riders ask before their first trip.

  • How do I request a ride?
    Open RideFlow, enter pickup and destination, choose your ride tier, and confirm.
  • How is pricing calculated?
    Fares use time, distance, and demand with an upfront estimate before booking.
  • Are RideFlow drivers verified?
    Yes. We run identity checks, background checks, and continuous quality monitoring.
  • How do I share trip progress?
    Use the in-app share option to send your ETA and live route to contacts.
  • What if I left an item in the vehicle?
    Report it in app support and we will coordinate with the assigned driver.
  • Can I use RideFlow internationally?
    Availability depends on city launch status and local regulation support.
